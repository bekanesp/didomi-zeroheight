### Kleos is the Didomi internal brand & design system. It is made of clearly defined rules and standards that communicate how our brand should be represented to the world. These guidelines help us ensure consistency and demonstrate what the company is, what it does, and what it stands for.

Like **Didomi**, a verb in ancient Greek that means Consent, the notion of **Kleos** (κλέος) goes back in time.

This term, used in ancient Greek epic poetry, means immortal fame and carries the implied meaning of renown or glory. The most closely related translation of the word Kleos is « what people say about you ».

A very important theme in Homer's great epics The Iliad and The Odyssey, Kleos often referred to having one's achievements venerated in poetry.

Like all great things, Kleos is not something that is given to you: you have to pursue it by actively executing the most honorable deeds.
