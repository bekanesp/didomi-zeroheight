# ChatGPT

Using Generative AI for content at Didomi.

Feel free to leverage ChatGPT when writing for work. It can be helpful with emails, social media posts, and even brainstorming.

However, when crafting public-facing communications, **never rely on ChatGPT to create a perfect final product**. You need to coach and prompt ChatGPT properly to make sure the output is up to par with our standards.

**⚠️ Do not trust ChatGPT with anything legal ⚠️**

Always check manually and/or run your output by your legal colleagues before publishing. ChatGPT will tell you wrong things with absolute confidence, especially regarding recent legal matters (we've seen it happen).

Just don't risk it.

## Didomi tone of voice prompt (copy this and paste it in ChatGPT)

To help you navigate the murky waters of generative AI, we've put together a prompt (a text with directions) you can provide ChatGPT when using it, to ensure it respects our tone of voice.

Send ChatGPT the following text (the whole thing at once) when starting a chat:

```
Going forward in this chat, I want your answers to respect my company's tone of voice requirements:

- Always use American English
- Didomi always capitalized
- Use active voice as much as possible
- When using an acronym, always spell it out at first, with the acronym in parentheses (ex: "California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)")
- Our product names are always capitalized, with the acronym in parentheses after (ex: Consent Management Platform (CMP), Preference Management Platform (PMP))
- Don't use a lot of exclamation marks.
- Don't use apostrophes when using my company name (Didomi). For example, don't say "Didomi's CMP" but instead say "The Didomi CMP"

Finally, always give me a warning whenever you're unsure about one of your answers. Being factual is of the utmost importance to me, and I need you to tell me whenever I should double-check anything with my team before sharing.
```

### ⚠️ Note on how long ChatGPT can remember stuff

In layman's terms, ChatGPT uses a "token" system to store and understand information. When you submit a prompt like the one above, it is able to remember and reference back to it in future responses, but only for a time.

It's good practice to use the prompt whenever you create a new work-related chat.
