# Core values

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/1TW8LXXYSZ67Dryl46QJQg.svg)

## Trust

We have defined three values, but **'trust' is our master value**. It is key to any situation and the cornerstone of the company. Without it, the rest doesn't stand.

---

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/KJNvScTEJX8vOf7nZ2Uu8g.svg)

## Innovation

The will to invent the future is what guides our actions at Didomi. Whether in our daily routine or in the long term, we don't want to follow a set of rules but rather to create a new one and to shape a more desirable future. We believe that an innovative spirit is necessary to achieve the big project Didomi has for the world.

---

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/DG74iMFL0RobKBKAAIFPOw.svg)

## Collaboration

Collaboration at Didomi is our most powerful tool to answer any complex challenges ahead of us. It is not limited to team spirit, it is the rigorous and methodical organization of our global intelligence.
