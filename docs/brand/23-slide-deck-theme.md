# Slide Deck Theme

A Google Slides theme is a predefined set of design elements that determine the overall look and feel of a Didomi presentation. Themes provide a consistent visual style across all of our decks, ensuring a cohesive and professional appearance.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/6M-B2l6bvGuNGbHRs5izaQ.png)

**The Didomi theme includes various design components:**

- Color schemes
- Slide layouts formats
- Font styles (**Montserrat** with different font weights is the only font to be used) you can refer to our [Brand Identity](https://kleos.didomi.io/62b372835/p/506208-typography) to learn more.

## **The easiest way to use a Didomi slide deck is to select an existing one from the library and copy it to your own Google Drive for editing.**

You start with an existing structure and simply modify the content to suit your needs, adding slides or deleting those you don't need.

### How?

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Log into your google account and select **"Slides"** |
| 2 | At the top of the page, you can see the Didomi Slide deck library. **Select an existing Didomi slide deck to get started** |
| 3 | On top left of the page, click on **File** and **Make a copy** |
| 4 | **Select your own drive space** to save the presentation. You are now ready to go. |

## **Would you prefer to start with a blank document and import the Didomi theme easily?**

### Follow the video tutorial or read the steps below

### Main 6 steps for importing a theme

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | **Create a new blank deck** on Google Slides |
| 2 | In the top navigation menu, go to: **Slide** > **Change Theme** |
| 3 | Click to **Import theme**, the yellow button in the menu window that opens on the right |
| 4 | Select in the list: **DIDOMI - Google Slides Template - 2023**. Feel free to use the search bar to filter the slide deck you need. |
| 5 | Click to **Import** and **Insert.** The Didomi theme is now available. |
| 6 | Click on the arrow **in the top left-hand corner next to the Menus button.** You now have access to all available slides to freely build your own Didomi presentation. |

## **Important things to remember when creating a slide deck to avoid hurting the designers**

### 1 / Always think simplicity and clarity

Keep your slides clean and uncluttered, with concise text and clear visuals.

Avoid overwhelming the audience with too much information on a single slide.

**Have a lot to say?**

- **Use bullet points** rather than full sentences
- **2 clear slides are better** than 1 cluttered slide
- **Highlight keywords** in bold or blue

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/qYiKF4bxxyy07Uvntoygqg.png)

### 2 / Maintaining visual consistency

Our theme's goal is to keep consistency across all of our slide decks. In order to maintain that consistency, here are a few things to keep in mind:

- Use our **unique font** Montserrat
- Avoid using multiple **font sizes**
- Keep **alignment and spacing** consistent across slides
- Stick to our **brand colors** only, they're all available directly from the theme colors
- Stick to our bank of icons & illustrations and **do not use random icons found online.**

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/hNSDMo5YkR2Z1OZ4oCa3AA.png)

### 3 / Select the right visuals to support your content

- Use images, charts, graphs, and videos that enhance your message and support your spoken words.
- Avoid irrelevant or distracting visuals. You will just end up confusing your audience.
- Prefer using branded visuals. **Visual library to be done**

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/_OvJnNA8pempOJQSar_wdw.png)

### 4 / Choose an audience-centric approach

Design your presentation with your audience's needs and interests in mind. Tailor the content to be **relevant and engaging for them.**

Put yourself in their shoes: what answer are they looking for? What problem are they trying to solve?

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/H5F6XSsNog5519SrpLFpZg.png)
