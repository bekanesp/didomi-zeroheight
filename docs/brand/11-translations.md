# Translations

In French, Spanish, German and Italian.

Didomi is a global company, and English is as a result our first language. However we do have a wide variety of languages spoken internally, and we **pride ourselves** in our ability to serve customers from all over the world.

When translating content and/or communicating in other languages, keep these best practices in mind and refer to the translation lexicon below for reference.

## Translation best practices

### Never translate product names

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| La Plateforme de Gestion des Consentements (CMP) | La Consent Management Platform (CMP) |

### When translating or including industry terms, include the original, English name for reference

If you need to translate an industry term to accommodate your audience, always refer to the official term between parentheses.

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| Outright including an English term, even though it's common practice in the industry. Ex: "Il est important de gérer ses vendors" | Include the original term in italics if it's an English term, and include the translation between parentheses (even if you only do it the first time it's mentioned). Ex: "Il est important de gérer ses vendors (partenaires)" |
