# Logotypes

## Main logo

### Bold and proud to be

The name Didomi stands on its own. The Didomi logo is deliberately free of any graphic code reflecting any industry, allowing the brand to express itself freely over time. Thus free, it aims to establish our positioning and leadership by reflecting a trusted brand.
