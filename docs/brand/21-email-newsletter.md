# Email & Newsletter

Didomi's email and newsletter are indispensable communication tools for internal communication, product communication and marketing communication. They are subject to visual structure rules that guarantee consistent communication across all distribution channels.

#### **Deployment for:**

- **Internal communication** (Company news, updates, highlights, data sharing...)
- **Brand communication** (Regulation news, Highlights, events, data sharing, webinars, newsletters, privacy market research...)
- **Product communication** (Product news, feature release, regulation updates, console management...)

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/xxTm2Pp93eDjpcoJzUIJdA.png)

---

## **How simplicity and clarity drives our email & newsletter design.**

Didomi email & newsletter designs share a common structure that allows us to express a set of needs without having to reinvent the wheel every time. We have axed the templates on simple frames allowing us fast communication and content deployment.

### The shell

The shell define the main structure of our emails & newsletter on desktop and mobile.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/mNg3Nf34gnti_o0FhQGpEA.png)

---

### The font

Because not all browsers and computers are capable of displaying our brand font, **Montserrat**, we have chosen a Google font, **Lato**, with numerous font weights and as close as possible to the original visual appearance of Montserrat.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/XNgsS_LH0xk3_Kfd7wTZwQ.png)

[![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/OCdePqoC6JBFx6wWnF3z6Q.png)](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Lato?query=lato)

### The components

A Figma component library defines the blocks we need. New components can be created for specific needs, but we strongly recommend not to create a new element if an existing block can accommodate the desired content.

> **_Most of the content of our e-mails and newsletters must be text-based to avoid problems with browsers downloading images._**

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/Cd7bvFHnnVyur3NdiypYdw.png)

### Color theme

Our emails and newsletters need to be able to adapt to the color management settings of our readers' browsers. We have therefore defined a dark theme based on our main identity, allowing us to maintain ease of reading and a sufficient level of contrast if a reader use a dark theme.
