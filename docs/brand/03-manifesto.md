# Manifesto

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/7EYR5A-9pwPH96_pTfBN9A.png)

## What we believe in and what we want for the world.

We want to build a world where people have regained control over the different technologies they use.

We want technology to be accountable and transparent, but that doesn't mean people shouldn't be either. Confidentiality and individual privacy must be respected and restored.

With that in mind, we oppose the model of total transparency and unbridled, uncontrolled exploitation of data. We want to stop the technological headlong rush that transforms our personal data into just another consumer product.

We want personal data to become a collective asset, freely shared by enlightened citizens, to solve the societal and planetary issues before us.

Thus, we set ourselves as protectors of people's confidentiality and builders of transparent technologies that respect their choices and rights, in particular regarding the processing of their personal data.

We support companies who, like us, believe that protecting the confidentiality of their customers is key to building trust, as well a lever for their economic growth. We do so by providing ways to build lasting, trust-based relationships between brands and users through privacy-first experiences.

**We are Didomi, we protect people's privacy and make data processing technologies more transparent.**
