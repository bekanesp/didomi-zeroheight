# Story

For years I have been intrigued by this proverb: **Trust but control.** My grandmother is my model for common sense, and I witnessed many times that she always rejected any form of technology that she didn't feel she could control.

Working in adtech, I realized in 2016 that this proverb may prove to be the key to the future of technology. I remember an Adage article back then titled *New EU Data Regulations will 'rip global digital ecosystem apart'*. Nobody paid attention at that time except everyone who took the time to read through the first versions of GDPR.

From there I ended up realizing the notion of consent (**Didomi** in ancient Greek) was going to be the solution people were looking for to control technology.

— **Romain Gauthier — Founder & Didomi CEO**
