# Website

The cornerstone of our new identity:

the website is an open window on Didomi's offer and vision.

---

## **Rich, comprehensive content that builds trust**

Our website enables us to offer prospects and readers visibility on all our user experience solutions available for privacy management. It also enables us to provide relevant legal content and documentation on regulatory developments around the world. We are also keen to highlight our most prestigious customers and their feedback through concrete client success stories.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/QHzy3fYw9Er3O8vi_5Vyfw.png)

---

### Product and solutions pages are built around a simple and same structure

Maintaining a simple design structure allows us to easily manage the amount of content, modifications and graphic additions, as well as facilitating backend updates.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/WCIrLTHqZS26p5Y1AOK2dg.png)

[![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/syaz24tECUZ4BzBTbW0y4g.png)](https://www.figma.com/file/ZGsUAGyoCvrLpZ2yITATfu/Website-Mockups-2023?type=design&node-id=0%3A1&mode=design&t=E7H1CrvCAn7IiYpz-1)

## **Let's have a closer look to the main blocks**

### The header

As the entry point to the website, it provides brand recognition. It's how users navigate the website. The header menu is deliberately designed to remain fixed on its main level during navigation, so that the user can access it at any time and easily keep an eye on the contact point or request a demo.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/s9q_5Pkt5Y2uaJCidrTu3g.png)

### The menus

Divided into different sections, the main menu allow the user to navigate through the Products and Solutions sections.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/Fg0zwN6OPnbzHderXM8pVA.png)

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/c8os8m85Jw1i2_EiifRBzw.png)

### The top header menu

The top header provides links to related content on privacy management and knowledge, such as blog articles, webinar series, guides and studies, and more. It also allow the user to log into the Didomi console, change the website language, access the partner program and learn more about our brand.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/6qcIhNsQzWkvqbbulNbZOA.png)

---

### Hero banner

#### These are the first visible banners, with a catchy title and striking visuals that set the tone!

First contact with the page, at a glance the user gets an idea of the page's topics.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/OKoqY8STFfZKIADwf7MyKw.png)

---

### Content blocks

#### These are blocks that display the content.

Built on two columns, content can be displayed alternately on the right or left. A set of 3 templates with different background colors, and a stronger visual emphasis on one of them, provide a rhythmic reading experience for the user.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/UqbibbykggW9VwaLko_reQ.png)

---

### Footer

#### The last block but not the least

An at-a-glance summary of everything you'll find on the website but also a contact touchpoint for Newsletter subscription and an easy access to our social media channels.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/ujbF96wHVYsKQOa4IWWgOg.png)

---

## **Responsiveness is something we value**

Our website is fully responsive, allowing us to offer optimal reading comfort and navigation from any display device. It also allows us to be visible at all moments and reach the largest number of readers and prospects.

![](https://kleos.didomi.io/uploads/M4PSlAJZmQ-cs5bvSEE1sA.png)
