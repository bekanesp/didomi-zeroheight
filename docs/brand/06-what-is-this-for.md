# What is this for?

Why do we need a tone of voice?

This tone of voice section is a guide for everyone at Didomi to refer to when speaking about us and our solutions.

## Why do we need a tone of voice?

- We're building the Didomi **brand**. So we need a consistent voice across all channels.
- We're aiming at **Enterprise** clients. So we need to speak like an Enterprise company.
- We're heading into the US and multinational markets. So the **quality** of our English needs to be impeccable.

*Even* if most of your day-to-day is carried out in another language, you still need to read this Guide, because the Enterprise and brand-building learnings can be applied to your communication.

Creating content and communicating with customers or prospects is not just the job of the content or marketing team.

**Every email, social media post, presentation, or message** must be aligned with our brand and, ultimately, with our objectives as a business.

## How does this align with our overall company objectives?

**Didomi's North Star is to maintain a company-wide focus on winning Enterprise clients.**

By building and implementing a cohesive tone of voice, we aim to deliver consistent Messaging/Communication/Experience to enterprise clients across the acquisition funnel.
