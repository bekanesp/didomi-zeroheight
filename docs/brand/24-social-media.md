### Basic visual elements

Design-wise, formats will vary depending on the platform and type of content. But although the structure of the posts can vary, some elements should always be present in our posts, such as **our logo, bold text elements, the use of our Authentic font to add a human touch or an illustration.**

In terms of formats, try and choose the ones that will occupy the most space on a mobile screen to catch the user's eye. Square is preferred to a landscape ratio.
