# How we talk about our solutions

Didomi Global Privacy UX Solutions and what they include.

*To learn more about our Global Privacy UX Solutions and understand how to talk about it, head to [the dedicated Notion page](https://www.notion.so/didomio/5de97e78735e426fadaeff2ae6e54017?v=a0b991b44cfc4da18eccdfd33a7424c6&pvs=4).*

To talk about Global Privacy UX Solutions include 3 core use cases and 3 added values that work together for our customers.

## 3 core use cases

### Multi-regulation consent management

**Features included:** *Cross-device, cross-domain, analytics, industry frameworks*

**What for?** Manage consent for privacy regulations around the world, streamlined across multiple user touchpoints.

**Value in a nutshell:** Display the right notice per geo, respect user consent, be able to prove it, and track consent analytics.

### Privacy governance

**Features included:** *Scanning, vendor & tracker analytics, alerting*

**What for?** Monitor vendor and tracker activity across hundreds of websites, effortlessly and automatically, staying compliant and reducing risk.

**Value in a nutshell:** display the right notice per geo, respect user consent, be able to prove it, and track consent analytics.

### User privacy journeys

**Features included:** *Preference management, DSAR, consent proofs*

**What for?** Reach out to customers beyond cookies, with the flexibility to compliantly manage user choices and requests at all stages of their journey.

**Value in a nutshell:** gather higher quality data and respond efficiently to user requests, generating trust, loyalty, and repeat business.

## 3 added values

### Integrations and connectors

**Included:** *Robust vendor marketplace*

**What is it?** Reliable, tested integrations for sharing user choices and consented data across the tool stack.

**Value in a nutshell:** independent, single source of truth for harmonizing user choices across all business tools, minimizing IT effort.

### Security and access management

**Included:** *ISO, SSO, RBAC*

**What is it?** Gold-standard measures for secure Privacy UX delivery, including ISO-27001 certification and dedicated User permissions.

**Value in a nutshell:** peace of mind that personal data management (including sensitive PII) is covered by best-in-class IT security.

### Premium services

**Included:** *Dedicated onboarding, consulting, and support*

**What is it?** Industry specialists in consent and privacy, at your service from implementation and throughout the entire customer journey.

**Value in a nutshell:** dedicated consent and compliance expertise, combined with specialized privacy tech support.
