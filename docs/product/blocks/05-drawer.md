# Drawer

Drawer is an unfolded panel allowing us to deploy relevant feature documentation or help management directly inside the console.

![Drawer overview](https://zeroheight-uploads.s3.eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/aeedcbf9bebc4d2017f225)

### Composition

You can see below most of the main elements contained in a Drawer. The structure is flexible and must be adapted to the content.

## Panel behavior

The Drawer appears when user clicks on the [Drawer push](https://kleos.didomi.io/62b372835/p/99d4dd-push) in the top right corner of the screen. On click, the panel comes in from the right of the screen, it passes over the content and remains on the right side of the screen.
