## Selection cards

**Selection cards are actionable cards that allow the user to make a single or multiple choice or to activate and deactivate features parameters for example.** These cards are mainly operable through checkboxes, radio buttons, toggle switches or simply by clicking on the card. The selection cards can be adapted to the needs and can contain different elements.
