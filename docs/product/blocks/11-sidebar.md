# Sidebar

**To accompany the vision of self-service,** we have defined a new sidebar component. The idea is to make navigation within the Didomi console more readable, more pleasant and above all more accessible by reducing the level of abstraction. **The navigation becomes a component that expands when hovering over its area and shows all the labels of the sections at a glance.**

### Placement

The sidebar remains on the left side of the screen by default. When the user hovers over the sidebar, it opens and displays all section labels. When open, the sidebar extends over the module area.
