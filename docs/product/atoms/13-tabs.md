# Tabs

Tabs organize groups of content. We use them to split content inside a feature **(Section tabs)** or to highlight a stepping flow **(Flow tabs). Sub level tabs** are used when the content and information of the architecture must be distributed to another sub-level due to the amount of content.

- [Section tabs](https://kleos.didomi.io/62b372835/p/3015bc-tabs/b/947d44)
- [Flow tabs](https://kleos.didomi.io/62b372835/p/3015bc-tabs/b/8029c5)
