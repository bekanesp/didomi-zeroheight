# Snackbars

Snackbars provide **brief messages** about console processes at the bottom of the screen. We use it to **eye catch quickly the user** on relevant and short informations.

## Error snackbars

**Error snackbars follow most of the same rules as snackbars.** The difference is that error snackbars always start with a short description/word in **bold** and then the reason for the error.
