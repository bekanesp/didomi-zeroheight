# Borders & Radius

## Borders

Border outlines are meant to **contain your content** generally sized between 1px to 4px

### Sizes & examples

![Border sizes](https://zeroheight-uploads.s3.eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/a78b7eace4da49cae72c51)

### Dividers

Dividers are tiny borders **used to divide, separate and prioritize content.** Separators are always set to 1px and can be solid lines or dashed lines.

![Dividers solid](https://zeroheight-uploads.s3.eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/11e5d412919a6f85cefe23)

![Dividers dashed](https://zeroheight-uploads.s3.eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/56140e5f4551ff82d4513c)

## Radius

Corner radius is the **corners of an element's outer border edge**, and is applied on button, cards, dropdowns, text fields etc… **they can range anywhere between 4px to 16px.**
