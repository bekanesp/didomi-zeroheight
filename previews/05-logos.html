Citing a sub-brand · two allowed modes
In running text
"Addingwell by Didomi is the server-side tagging platform."
Italicise by; it never takes the capital. Use when typography is the hero and a logo would be visual noise.
As a logo / lockup
Place the artwork from the library. Never re-typeset the mark.
Use on hero panels, product UI, and anywhere branding is the anchor. Pair with clear space of at least the mark's x-height.
The D in Didomi is always capitalised. Sub-brand lockups read Addingwell by Didomi and Sourcepoint by Didomi, with lowercase by baked into the artwork. Preserve the SVGs; never re-typeset the wordmark.
