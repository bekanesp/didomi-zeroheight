The gradient is an added element on a background.

Treat it like atmosphere · a soft, blurred accent on a navy or off-white surface. Never use it as a fill · not on text, not on buttons, not inside shapes, not as a body-copy background. Solid Electric Blue, Dark Navy and Off-white do all of that work.
